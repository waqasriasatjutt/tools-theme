Tip — common QR formats

  • URL: just paste the address: https://example.com
  • WiFi: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;
  • Contact (vCard): BEGIN:VCARD\nVERSION:3.0\nFN:Jane Doe\nTEL:+1234567890\nEND:VCARD
  • Email: mailto:hello@example.com?subject=Hi
  • SMS: sms:+1234567890?body=Hello
